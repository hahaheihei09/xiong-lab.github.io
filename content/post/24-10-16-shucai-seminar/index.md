On October 16-17, 2024, the "Smart Technology and Equipment Empowering High-Quality Development of the Vegetable Industry" seminar was successfully held in Beijing, under the guidance of the Beijing Association for Science and Technology and hosted by the Beijing Agricultural Association, Beijing Academy of Agriculture and Forestry Sciences, and Beijing Vegetable Association. 

Dr. Ya Xiong was invited to participate in the event, where he delivered a report titled "Vegetable Harvesting Robots and Their Key Technologies." A group of experts and scholars from the vegetable industry across China attended the conference to discuss cutting-edge innovations in smart technologies and equipment, seeking new paths for technology to promote high-quality agricultural development.
