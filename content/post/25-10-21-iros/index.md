---
title: "S-H Robotics Lab Co-organizes Agrirobotics Workshop at IROS 2025"
date: 2025-10-21
---

On October 20, 2025, the **IROS Workshop on Agricultural Robotics (Agrirobotics)**, co-organized by **Dr. Ya Xiong** alongside leading experts from Wageningen University, University of Lincoln, and Northwest A&F University, was successfully held at the top-tier robotics conference **IROS**.

The workshop focused on **"Perception and Manipulation in Complex and Dynamic Agricultural Environments."** It featured keynote speeches, paper presentations, and panel discussions with participants from global institutions including Cambridge University, Carnegie Mellon University, UC Davis, and Zhejiang University. Awards for **Best Scientific Contribution** and **Best Engineering Application** were presented.

This event provided a vital platform for global innovation and collaboration in agricultural robotics, highlighting our lab's active role in advancing the field.
